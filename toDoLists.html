To do lists

What to do when you are feeling anxious, depressed, angry and so on? We are having some To do lists to help you navigate through these emotions.

Anxiety

1. Take deep breaths.

2. Go for a walk.

3. Listen to calming music.

4. Write down your thoughts.

5. Practice mindfulness or meditation.

Depression

1. Reach out to a friend.

2. Engage in a hobby.

3. Write a journal.

4. Seek professional help.

5. Practice mindfulness or meditation.

Anger

1. Count to ten before reacting.

2. Take a break from the situation.

3. Write down your feelings.

4. Engage in physical activity.

5. Practice deep breathing exercises.

Stress

1. Identify the source of stress.

2. Practice time management.

3. Take breaks and relax.

4. Engage in physical activity.

5. Practice deep breathing exercises.
